# Day 13–14: Neural Networks & NLP

## Neural Networks

Today I learned the basic idea of neural networks. They are inspired by how the human brain works.

A neural network has:
- Input layer
- Hidden layers
- Output layer

The main idea is that data passes through these layers, and the model adjusts something called "weights" to improve its predictions.

I understood that:
- Everything is converted into numbers
- The model learns by reducing error over time

---

## NLP (Natural Language Processing)

NLP is about helping computers understand human language.

Examples:
- Chatbots
- Translation (like Google Translate)
- Spam detection

The process is something like:
text → split into words → convert to numbers → model processes → output

Neural networks are the technique used to learn patterns, and NLP is one area where they are applied.
